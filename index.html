Phirni Recipe

Phirni also called as firni is a classic Indian sweet pudding that is a must in North India on festive occasions or special celebrations like Diwali as well as for Karwa Chauth Festival. Try my classic and traditional Phirni recipe and I am sure it will become your new favorite Indian sweet.Phirni is a thick and creamy pudding made from ground rice. To make the simplest and yet the best phirni recipe all you need is rice, milk, sugar, nuts and saffron.
